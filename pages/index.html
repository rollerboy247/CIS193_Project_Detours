San Francisco

San Francisco! San Fran. But not Frisco. Never call it Frisco, otherwise you might get a steaming hot cup of organic dark roast Blue Bottle coffee thrown in your face. Join us on this unique tour of The City by the Bay as we explore its ups and downs, twists and turns, favorite sights and hidden gems, all on one of SF's hippest and most iconic modes of transport... the fixed-gear bicycle! Our route begins at the Embarcadero Center Farmer's Market where, after noshing on a Tartine morning bun, we'll race alongside streetcars around the wharf. Then, it's calf-crunching time as we trek up Hyde street and blow kisses to the cable car riders. At the summit, we'll soak in the views of Alcatraz, the Golden Gate Bridge, and Lombard Street. Then, off to lunch at Frankie's Bohemian for a gut-punching brambory! From there, it's pedal pedal pedal to Amoeba Records in Haight-Ashbury, where you can pick up some vinyl and get some body piercings. Afterwards, a brisk ride up Twin Peaks (1,500 foot climb, no whining allowed), where we'll take in a full panoramic view of the entire city while sipping mojitos! And to complete the day, we'll race downhill to dinner in the Mission at the vegan mecca, Gracias Madre.
